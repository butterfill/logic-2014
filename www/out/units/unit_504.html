All Squares Are Blue (Fast Version)

\begin{minipage}{\columnwidth}
\emph{∃ and ∧ work together}
Some square is blue:
∃x ( Square(x) ∧ Blue(x) )
Some of my things are broken:
∃x ( Belongs(a,x) ∧ Broken(x) )
\end{minipage}
\begin{minipage}{\columnwidth}
\emph{∀ and → work together}
All squares are blue:
∀x ( Square(x) → Blue(x) )
All my things are broken:
∀x ( Belongs(a,x) → Broken(x) )
\end{minipage}
