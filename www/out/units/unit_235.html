DeMorgan: ¬(A ∧ B) ⫤⊨ ¬A ∨ ¬B

`$\leftmodels\models$' means `is logically equivalent to', so for now `has the same truth table as'.
A $\leftmodels\models$ ¬¬A
¬(A ∧ B) $\leftmodels\models$ (¬A ∨ ¬B)
¬(A ∨ B) $\leftmodels\models$ (¬A ∧ ¬B)
A → B $\leftmodels\models$ ¬A ∨ B
¬(A → B) $\leftmodels\models$ ¬(¬A ∨ B) $\leftmodels\models$ A ∧ ¬B
